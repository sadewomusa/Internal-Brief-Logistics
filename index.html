Iran has struck Fujairah's oil terminals repeatedly throughout March 2026. Force majeure declared. Operations suspended. ⚠️ Special Consideration — Political & Security Context

The Geopolitical Dimension

Sharjah's political positioning gives KCT a distinct threat profile. This is a real operational factor — not a formality — and it must inform infrastructure decisions, investor selection, and public communications. GT controls how it builds, who it partners with, and what narrative it puts into the market. Those are the levers.

Infrastructure cannot be disguised. Floating roof tanks, VLCC berths, manifold piping — a trained analyst identifies these from satellite imagery or a single site visit. The physical signature determines the threat profile. Not the name. Not the license category.
